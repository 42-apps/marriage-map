# Marriage Map

An interactive **3D globe** (and flat map) that colours every country by a chosen dimension of
marriage — divorce rate, children per woman, share of adults married, marriage rate, age at first
marriage, minimum legal age, child marriage, polygamy, or same-sex marriage — beside a scrubbable
**history of marriage** from the first written marriage law to today.

### ▶ Live: https://42-apps.github.io/marriage-map/

- **223 countries & territories**, 10 switchable perspectives with adaptive colour scales
- Click any country for its full marriage profile; live ranked country list
- **152-milestone** history-of-marriage timeline (3000 BCE → today) with sources, plus global trends
- Globe or flat map, country search, shareable deep-link views — no accounts, no tracking, runs in your browser

A [42-apps](https://42-apps.github.io/) mini-site, forked from the World Religions / Jewish History
globe engine. Data: UN DESA World Marriage Data, OECD Family Database, World Bank, Pew, Our World in
Data, Eurostat. Borders: Natural Earth. Globe: globe.gl.
