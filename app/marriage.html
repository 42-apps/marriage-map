Marriage Map

An interactive map of marriage around the world — and a timeline of marriage through history. Colour the globe by any perspective: divorce rate, children per woman, share of adults married, marriage rate, age at marriage, the minimum legal age, child marriage, polygamy, or same-sex marriage. Click any country for its full marriage profile.

Below, scrub the history of marriage — from the earliest recorded marriage law in Mesopotamia to civil marriage, women's property rights, no-fault divorce and marriage equality — and see where each milestone first happened.

Please read: figures are the latest reliable estimates and definitions vary between countries (e.g. “divorce rate” can be measured several ways). Treat cross-country comparisons as indicative, not exact.

Sources: UN DESA (World Marriage Data) · OECD Family Database · World Bank · Pew Research · Our World in Data · Eurostat & national statistics. Borders: Natural Earth. Globe: globe.gl.
